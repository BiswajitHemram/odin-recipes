Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a beloved and flavorful rice dish that originates from the Indian subcontinent

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter) or vegetable oil
  • Whole spices (cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, bay leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron strands (optional, for garnish)
  • Fried onions (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Add the whole spices and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  7. Stir in the red chili powder, biryani masala powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Add the plain yogurt, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Mix everything together.
  9. Pour in the water and bring it to a boil.
  10. Add the soaked and drained basmati rice to the boiling water. Gently mix to combine.
  11. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the flavors are absorbed.
  12. Once cooked, remove from heat and let it sit covered for 10 minutes.
  13. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
  14. Garnish with saffron strands and fried onions, if desired.
  15. Serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) or plain yogurt on the side.

Enjoy the aromatic and flavorful Chicken Biryani!
